Frequently Asked Questions

What if I get a flat tire? v
Call us at 0800 wheels2go and we'll get you set up with new wheels and add 30 mins to your time to account for time missed
What if it starts to rain and my plans change? v
If your plans change (no matter the reason), simply go to your email order confirmation and click ‘Cancel my order’. Provided you have not yet picked up your wheels, this will automatically refund you and deactivate the code. If you have already picked up your wheels, call us at 0800 wheels2go and we’ll sort something out depending on the situation
Am I covered by insurance if I get into an accident? v
Yes. Email us at wheels2go.is@gmail.com explaining the situation and we'll get it all sorted for you
What does 'Kid-Sized' mean? v
Kid-Sized gets you a medium sized bike suitable for an average 7-10 year old.
Why do I need to register? v
Registering means its a lot easier for us to keep track of your details and keep a history of past purchases. This is useful when things go wrong, allows us to group purchases made at the same time, and also gives you the occasional discount!
